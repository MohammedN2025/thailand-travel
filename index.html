Trip Highlights

A quick snapshot of what makes Thailand special.

🏛️ Glittering Temples

Marvel at the Grand Palace, Wat Pho’s reclining Buddha, and riverside Wat Arun at sunset.

🏝️ Tropical Beaches

Island‑hop around Phuket, Krabi, and Koh Phi Phi — limestone cliffs, turquoise water, and powdery sand.

🍜 Legendary Street Food

Pad Thai, green curry, mango sticky rice — best enjoyed at bustling night markets and street stalls.

Top Destinations

Mix city energy with serene temples and island time.

Culture & Food

Traditions & Festivals

  • Songkran (Thai New Year): nationwide water festivities each April.
  • Loy Krathong & Yi Peng: floating offerings and lantern‑lit skies in November.
  • Temple Etiquette: dress modestly; remove shoes; be respectful around monks.

Cuisine

  • Street Classics: Pad Thai, Som Tam (papaya salad), Mango Sticky Rice.
  • Curry Favourites: Green, Red, and Massaman curries with fragrant jasmine rice.
  • Market Drinks: Thai iced tea & fresh coconut.

Travel Tips

🗓️ Best Time

Cool & dry season from November to February offers pleasant weather across most regions.

🚇 Getting Around

Use BTS/MRT in Bangkok; tuk‑tuks for short hops (agree price first); ferries for river and islands.

🙏 Temple Dress Code

Cover shoulders & knees; carry a light scarf. Remove shoes before entering temple buildings.

💡 Smart & Safe

Use official taxis or apps; beware common scams; stay hydrated; carry small cash for markets.
